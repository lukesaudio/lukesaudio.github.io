About

Hey, I'm Luke. I'm a Creative Music Technology graduate with keen interest in video game audio. I joined university as a performing vocalist and guitarist and quickly transitioned into music and audio design for video games. I have a love of generative and dynamic audio systems, and believe they have a key importance in influencing a player's experience of a video game. My undergraduate dissertation Exploring Adaptive Reverberation in Video Games introduced me to the concept of 'intelligent' audio systems in video games, which is now a topic I am carrying on in my postgraduate studies. I hope to make new and memorable audio experiences in interactive environments that go beyond simply writing dynamically changing music.
I currently live in Bristol with my partner, Paige, an MSc Events Management student also at UWE. I am a huge fan of the band Biffy Clyro; their weirdness, complexty and ability to innovate in music is an ethos I imbue into my own work. Finally, I love a good burger and whiskey, and run a blog following my escapades into such.
