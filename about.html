Who the eff is Luke Child?

Luke Child is a PhD Student, Lecturer and Creative Technologist. Luke is a White male with a bushy beard, quiffed short hair and glasses. He usually dons something colourful and abstract, with fashionable items ranging from neon cat Yaks to every piece of contemporary Biffy Clyro merch available.

Originally from Worcester, home of the famous mispronounced sauce, he now operates out of Bristol, UK. Luke is working towards a PhD in Accessible Sonic Interaction Design in Virtual Environments and collaborates with undergraduate students working on research methods, audio programming, and accessible design.

A big fan of weird and wonderful things, Luke likes music that ventures into experimental territories (particularly rhythmic), patching together unconventional signal chains, and making and breaking things in the quest for surprising outcomes.

He is almost always listening to Biffy Clyro, very much loves Corgis and befriends them at a moments notice and, like all academics, disguises a caffeine addiction as 'a love for the artisinal craft that is coffee'.
