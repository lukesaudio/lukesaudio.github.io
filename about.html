Luke is an audio researcher, musician and creative working in Bristol, UK

He grew up as a wee lad in Worcestershire, in the West Midlands where, as an angsty teen, he learned guitar and began writing math-infused alternative rock after becoming obsessed with the band Biffy Clyro.

He ventured to Bristol to study Creative Music Technology, developing an interest in game audio and digital reverberation. He also formed the band 'Scrub' in this time, a tongue-in-cheek punk project writing songs about wacky occurrances, such as dancing old ladies, drag racing mothers and barista girlfriends.

These studies led him on to a Masters degree in Creative Technology, where Luke became invested in audio and accessibility, particularly the ways in which reverberation is percieved. In this time, he also started the spacey alt-hop pseudonym 'Cosmic' on independent label 'Satori Records', releasing his first EP 'Gravitate' in February 2020.

Luke is currently writing papers and proposals towards starting a PhD in Audio Perception and Accessibility.

Away from these things, Luke is a pretty neighbourly dude. He enjoys making generative art, drinking Japanese whisky, LOVES corgis, and finds 4/4 unnerving, much preferring the finer palate of a good 5/4 with a dash of jazzy semblance to taste.
