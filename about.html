About Me

I am an audio researcher, musician and creative technologist who is a fiercely proud West Midlander at heart, but now resides in Bristol, UK

I got into music through listening to Foo Fighters, Biffy Clyro and Trivium endlessly in my younger years, trying to learn guitar parts so that I could jam along

When I became an angsty emo teen, I started writing and recording my own music, before embarking on Creative Music Technology BSc. I transitioned my love of audio into a new found love of research through an MSc in Creative Technology.

I now get stuck in with all things audio perception, audio accessibility and interactive audio, and am actively researching in these areas

Away from this, you'll find me messing around with weird time signatures, playing board games, or assembling plugin chains that would make an mix engineer cry
