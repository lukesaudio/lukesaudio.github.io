Hey folks, I'm Luke! I'm a musician, sound design and creative technologies graduate out of Bristol, UK. I love everything weird and wonderful in life and use this to influence my work. I'm partial to an irregular time signature, a jazz chord, abstract art and projects that break the boundaries between smartly successful and wonderfully whimsical. My creative ethos is that every idea is worth exploring if you are prepraed to do it. I've made projects involving automated band fighting simulators in a pit of lava, designed sounds featuring my partner singing as if she were a toddler, and wrote music about a friend's ex-girlfriend's mother being a badass who drag races cars (100% based on a true story). Away from this, I'm an avid whiskey drinker, think corgis are the best dog breed, listen almost exclusively to Biffy Clyro, and run a blog on tasty burgers I find throughout my travels.
