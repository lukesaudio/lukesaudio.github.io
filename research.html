Here you'll find the ACTUAL research I do...

Audio interaction, accessibility, video games, and a BOATLOAD of coffee


The research I do delves into the lower echelons of audio interaction in interactive mediums. What does sound afford us? What do we hear similarly? What is different? How can we harness this to build new experiences in the digital realm? How can we talk about sound when it isn't accessible? Lots of questions are asked here, but we have MEGA fun on the journey through exploring them.
