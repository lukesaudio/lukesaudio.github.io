AdVerb: Adaptive Reverberation for Unity

AdVerb is a reverberation system I am developing for the Unity games engine. AdVerb is a portmanteau of Adaptive and Reverb, and is a system that adapts to a given world space within a video game, and creates a reverberation profile accordingly. The system uses raycasting and shader properties to compute averages for values such as surface smoothness, metallicness and proximity, and then applies these to an in-Unity reverb filter at runtime.

This was developed for my undergraduate dissertation. You can access the dissertation HERE
