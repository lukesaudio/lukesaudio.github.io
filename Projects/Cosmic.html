'Cosmic' is a music project pseudonym I work under, producing trip-hop, psychedelic and lo-fi music through Satori Records.

The music is experimental and mathy, features some spicy composition and lots of drone and ambience backdrops. I also love a good low-register voice/digitised autotuned vocal.

I have released one 3-track EP so far titled 'Gravitate' which came out on the 7th of Februray 2020. You can find purchase/download links to it here.
