'Scrub' is a semi-serious folk punk project ran by myself and Corey Ford, a great friend of mine from univeristy. My love of folk and sarcastic songwriting, with Corey's love of punk and being a bit silly formed our questionable act.

Though we have no official releases currently, we plan to record at the next convenience (thanks Covid-19). On that release, you'll find songs about me being the president of the USA, about a friend of mine's ex girlfriend's mum drag racing cars, about an 86 year old dancing lady, and my girlfriend the barista.
