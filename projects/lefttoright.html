//projects -> left to right

'Left to Right' was an art outcome spawned from a pencil and paper rule-centric exercise. I was tasked with creating a simple ruleset for drawing lines on paper and convertiing the result into code using the openFrameworks c++ library. The ruleset here was originally to draw segmented lines of a designated length from the left side of the screen to the right at a random degree offset, and to do this multiple times.

Due to some ahem... features present in the code, as well as some fiddling around with automation and filling, this wonderus result was discovered!
