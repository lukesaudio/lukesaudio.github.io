Luke teaches things?

Yes, and only the coolest of things. My teaching is split between three primary areas: audio programming, research methods, and project supervision. I strive to create collaborative and dialogic partnerships with my students, considering learning and teaching as symbiotic and facilitated by both sides. I endeavour to learn as much as my students alongside them and adapt to everyone's unique styles of learning, while bringing along a sprinkle of seredipitous wonder and weirdness.

I encourage students to consider accessibility and inclusive design in their projects, regardless of topic. It is my hope that new graduates are aware that designing for all is an awesome thing to do, but should also be the standard for all industries instead of an afterthought, selling point, or checkbox to tick.
