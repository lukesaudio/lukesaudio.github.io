Cosmic



Cosmic is the pseudonym under which I produce polyrhythmic, jazzy space-infused rap and triphop



This project's music is released through Satori Records


This project was conceptualised during my third year Composition module at uni, in which I wrote the track 'Gravitate' for an electro-acoustic assignment. My lecturer quite liked it.

Flash forward to end of year 2019, my good buddy Kuriboh said he was looking for artists to jump on his label 'Satori Records'. I sent him a couple track ideas I'd been working on, and t e rest is history.

My first release was an EP called 'Gravitate'



It featured three songs: Gravitate, Glue and Scream

You can listen/purchase the EP on Bandcamp and Spotify
