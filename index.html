About Me

Hey. I'm Luke, a Creative Music Technology graduate from UWE interested in sound design and audio programming for video games!

I came to uni as a performing musician, and over three years transitioned into making audio for video games. I have a liking for the unusual, complex and strange in audio and music, and this comes across in the works I produce.

I'm a devoted fan of Biffy Clyro, corgis, burgers, and a good whiskey. Hope you enjoy what ya find on here. Please get in touch if you want to chat!
