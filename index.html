So who the 'eff is Luke?

Hey there, friend! My name is Luke. I use he/him pronouns. I am a PhD researcher, maker and musician originally from the West Midlands and currently residing in Bristol, UK. I'm a White British male with unkempt middle parted hair, a dark beard and stasche combo, and usually wearing glasses and a big smile. Research and Education

I am a PhD Researcher and teacehr somewhere at the crossroads of audio, games and accessibility. I love games with open world navigation, but they're often designed to be visual-first experiences, such as reading maps, following a path, or finding an awesome chest in a cave, only to find out that it's a f*cking mimic... But why not do that with sound? What does auditory navigation sound like in games? That's where I'm building stuff.
